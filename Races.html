RACES

Dwarf

Ability score increase: Constitution +2
Age: On average they live up to 350 years.
Size Between 4 and 5 feet tall. Weigh around 150 pounds.
Speed: 25 feet.
Languages: You know Common and Dwarvish
Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light.
Dwarven Resilience: You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage.
Dwarven combat training: You are proficient with the battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer and warhammer.
Tool proficiency: You gain proficiency with smith's tools, brewer's supplies, or mason's tools.
Stonecunning: When making a history check related to the origin of stonework, add double your proficiency bonus.

Subraces
Hill Dwarf
Ability score increase: Strength +2
Dwarven Toughness: Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.

Mountain Dwarf
Ability score increase: Strength +2
Dwarven Armour Training: You are proficient with light and medium armour.

Elf

Ability score increase: Dexterity +2
Age: Elves can live up to 750 years.
Size: Between 4 and 7 feet tall.
Speed: 30 feet.
Languages: You know Common and Elvish
Darkvision:You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light.
Keen Senses: You are proficient in perception.
Fey Ancestry:You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
Trance: Elves do not sleep instead they meditate for 4

Subraces
High-elf
Ability score increase: Intelligence +1
Elf weapon training: You have proficiency with longswords, shortswords, shortbows and longbows.
Cantrip: You know one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list.
Extra language: You know one extra language of your choice.

Wood-elf
Ability score increase: Wisdom +1
Elf weapon training: You have proficiency with longswords, shortswords, shortbows and longbows.
Fleet of foot: Your base walking speed increases to 35 feet.
Mask of the wild: You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist and other natural phenomena.

Dark-elf (Drow)
Ability Score increase: Charisma +1
Superior Darkvision: Your darkvision has a radius of 120 feet.
Sunlight sensitivity: You have disadvantage on attack rolls and perception checks when you, your target or what you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.
Drow weapon training: You have proficiency with rapiers, shortswords, and hand crossbows.
Drow magic: You know the dancing lights cantrip at, can cast the faerie fire spell at 3rd level and at 5th level you can cast the darkness once per long rest.

Human

Ability score increase: All stats +1
Age:Humans live for less than a century.
Size: From around 5 up to 7 feet tall.
Speed: 30 feet.
Languages:Common and a language of your choice.

Half-elf

Ability score increase: Charisma +2 and +1 to two of your choice.
Age: Half-elves can live up to 180 years.
Size: Ranges from 5 to 6 feet tall.
Speed: 30 feet
Languages: Common, Elvish and a language of your choice.
Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light.
Fey ancestry: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic cannot put you to sleep.
Skill versatility: You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice.

Half-orc

Ability score increase: Strength +2 and constitution +1
Age: Half-orcs can live up to 75 years.
Size: Can be 5 to 7 feet tall.
Speed: 30 feet.
Languages: Common and Orc.
Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light.
Menacing: You gain proficiency in intimidation.
Relentless endurance: Once per long rest. When you are reduced to 1 hit point but not outright killed, you can drop to 1 hit point instead.
Savage Attacks: When you score a critical hit you can roll an extra damage dice for the damage.

Halfling

Ability score increase: Dexterity +2
Age: Halflings can live up to 160 years.
Size: Halflings are around 3 feet tall.
Speed: 25 feet.
Languages: Common and Halfling.
Lucky: When you roll a 1 on a d20 for an attack, ability check or saving throw you can reroll and use the new number.
Brave: You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
Halfling Nimbleness: You can move through the space of any creature that is larger than you.

Subraces
Lightfoot
Ability score increase: Charisma +1
Naturally stealthy: You can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is larger than you.

Stout
Ability score increase: Constitution +1
Stout resilience: You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and resistance against poison damage.

Gnome

Ability score increase: Intelligence +2
Age: Gnomes can live up to 500 years.
Size: Between 3 to 4 feet tall.
Speed: 25 feet.
Languages: Common and Gnomish.

Subraces
Forest Gnome
Ability score increase: Dexterity +1
Natural illusionist: You know the minor illusion cantrip.
Speak with small beasts: You are able to communicate simple ideas with small beasts. Forest gnomes love animals and often keep small woodland animals as pets.

Rock
Ability score increase: Constitution +1
Artificer's lore: When making a history check related to magic items, alchemic objects or technological devices, you can double your proficiency bonus.
Tinker: You are proficient with artisan's tools and are able to construct a clockwork device that works for 24 hours. This device can be a clockwork toy, a fire starter or a music box.

Tiefling

Ability score increase: Intelligence +1 and charisma +2
Age: Tieflings can live for around 100 years.
Size: Their height can range from around 5 feet up to 7 feet.
Speed: 30 feet.
Languages: Common and infernal
Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light.
Hellish Resistance: You have resistance to fire damage.
Infernal Legacy: You know the thaumaturgy cantrip at 3rd level, can cast hellish rebuke as a 2nd-level spell at 5th level and can cast the darkness spell once per long rest.

Dragonborn

Ability score increase: Strength +2 and charisma +1
Age: Dragonborns live up to 80 years.
Size: Can be as tall as 8 feet.
Speed: 30 feet
Languages: Common and Draconic.
Draconic ancestry: Choose a type of dragon from the table below, this determines your damage resistance and breath weapon.
Damage resistance: You have resistance to the damage type associated with your draconic ancestry.
Breath weapon: You can use your action to exhale either acid, fire, lightning, poison or ice depending on your dragon ancestry. The DC for the saving throw is 8+ your constitution modifier + your proficiency bonus. The attack does 2d6 damage (half on a successful save). The damage increases to 3d6 at 6th level, 4d6 at 11th level and 5d6 at 16th level.
