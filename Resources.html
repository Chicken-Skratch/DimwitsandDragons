If you don't know how to fill in a character sheet, here's how.

First choose a race and class.
These are listed in their respective webpages.

Now let's get the six main statistics. To get these you need to roll four six-sided dice.
Then add up the three largest numbers of those four dice.
Do this six times.
Once you have these six numbers you can then decide where you want to put these numbers in this part of the character sheet.

Now to work out the bonus for each stat by using this table.


Then you can fill in this part by colouring in each circle by the skills you are proficient in.


You can find out what skills you are proficient in by going to the classes page.
Then on the line beside each stat you put the ability modifier and add the proficiency bonus if necessary.

Use this table to figure out your proficiency bonus.


Next we'll fill in your passive perception, armour class and initiative modifier.
These are located here on the character sheet.


Your passive perception is ten plus your wisdom modifier.
Your armour class is ten plus your dexterity, this can be increased by using armour.
Your initiative modifier is your dexterity modifier, this is then added onto your initiative roll when you play.

Now we'll fill in your speed, hit points and hit dice.

Your speed depends on what race your character is. You can choose one in the races page.

Your hit points and hit dice depend on what class you have chosen. You can choose a class in the classes page.

Finally, we will fill in your attack bonuses for each weapon you have. We fill this in here.

You can choose different weapons that are available to your class, these are listed in the classes page. Each weapon is associated with either strength or dexterity.
You add the ability modifier associated with your weapon and your proficiency bonus together, that is your attack bonus for your weapon.

Now you have the complicated part of your character sheet filled in. The rest is up to you.
