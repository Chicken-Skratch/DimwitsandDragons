CLASSES

Barbarian

Barbarians are fierce warriors of primitive background who can enter a battle rage.
Hit dice: 1d12 per barbarian level.
Hit points at first level: 12+ constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d12(or 7)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: light armour, medium armour, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons.
Saving throws: Strength and constitution.
Skills: Choose two from animal handling, athletics, intimidation, nature, perception and survival.
Equipment:A great axe or any martial melee weapon. Two handaxes or any simple weapon. An explorers pack and four javelins.
Funds:2d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Bard


Bards are inspiring magicians whose power echoes the music of creation.
Hit dice: 1d8 per bard level.
Hit points at first level: 8+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8(or 5)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: light armour, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords, three musical instruments. of your choice
Saving throws: Dexterity and charisma.
Skills: Choose any three.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Cantrips and spells: At 1st level you know two cantrips, four spells and have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: A rapier, a longsword or any simple weapon. A diplomat's pack or an entertainer's pack. A lute or any other musical instrument. Leather armour and a dagger.
Funds:5d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Cleric


Clerics are priestly champions who wield divine magic in service of a higher power.
Hit dice: 1d8 per cleric level.
Hit points at first level: 8+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8(or 5)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: light armour, medium armour, shields, simple weapons.
Saving throws: Wisdom and charisma.
Skills: Choose two from history, insight, medicine, persuasion, and religion.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier.
Cantrips and spells:At 1st level you know three cantrips and have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: A mace or a warhammer. Scalemail, leather armour or chain mail. A light crossbow and 20 bolts or any simple weapon. A priest’s pack or an explorer's pack. A shield and a holy symbol.
Funds:5d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Druid


Druids are priests of old faith, they wield the powers of nature-moonlight and plant growth, fire and lightning-and adopting animal forms.
Hit dice: 1d8 per druid level.
Hit points at first level: 8+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8(or 5)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: light armour, medium armour, shields (druids will not wear armour or use shields made of metal), clubs daggers, darts, javelins, maces, quarterstaffs, scimitars, sickles, slings, spears, herbalism kit.
Saving throws: Intelligence and wisdom.
Skills: Choose two from arcana, animal handling, insight, medicine, nature, perception, religion, and survival.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier
Cantrips and spells: At 1st level you know two cantrips and have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: A wooden shield or any simple weapon. A scimitar or any simple melee weapon. Leather armour, an explorer's pack and a druidic focus.
Funds: 2d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Fighter


Fighters are masters of martial combat, skilled with a variety of weapons and armour.
Hit dice: 1d10 per fighter level.
Hit points at first level: 10+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d10(or 6)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: all armour, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons.
Saving throws: Strength and constitution.
Skills: Choose two from acrobatics, animal handling, athletics, history, insight, intimidation, perception, and survival.
Equipment: Chain mail or leather armour, longbow, and 20 arrows. A martial weapon and a shield or two martial weapons. A light crossbow and 20 bolts or two handaxes. A dungeoneer's pack or an explorer's pack.
Funds: 5d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Monk


Monks are masters of martial arts, harnessing the power of the body in pursuit of physical and spiritual perfection.
Hit dice: 1d8 per monk level.
Hit points at first level: 8+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8(or 5)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: Simple weapons, shortswords, choose a type of artisan's tools or a musical instrument.
Saving throws: Strength and dexterity.
Skills: Choose two from acrobatics, athletics, history, insight, religion, and stealth.
Equipment: A shortsword or any simple weapon. A dungeoneer's pack or an explorer's pack. 10 darts.
Funds: 5d4 = amount of gold pieces.

Paladin


Paladins are holy warriors bound to a sacred oath.
Hit dice: 1d10 per paladin level.
Hit points at first level: 10+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d10(or 6)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: All armour, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons.
Saving throws: Wisdom and charisma.
Skills: Choose two from athletics, insight, intimidation, medicine, persuasion, and religion.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Spells: At 2nd level you have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: A martial weapon and a shield or two martial weapons. Five javelins or any simple melee weapon. A priest's pack or an explorer's pack. Chain mail and a holy symbol.
Funds: 5d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Ranger


Rangers are warriors who use martial prowess and nature magic to combat threats on the edges of civilization.
Hit dice: 1d10 per ranger level.
Hit points at first level: 10+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d10(or 6)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: light armour, medium armour, shields, simple weapons, martial weapons.
Saving throws: Strength and dexterity.
Skills: Choose three from animal handling, athletics, insight, investigation, nature, perception, stealth, and survival.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier.
Spells: At 2nd level you know two spells and have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: Scale mail or leather armour. Two shortswords or two simple melee weapons. A dungeoneer's pack or an explorer's pack. A longbow and a quiver of 20 arrows.
Funds: 5d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Rogue


Rouges are scoundrels who use stealth and trickery to overcome obstacles and enemies.
Hit dice: 1d8 per rogue level.
Hit points at first level: 8+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8(or 5)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: Light armour, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords, thieves' tool.
Saving throws: Dexterity and intelligence.
Skills: Choose four from acrobatics, athletics, deception, insight, intimidation, investigation, perception, performance, persuasion, sleight of hand, and stealth.
Equipment: A rapier or a shortsword. A shortbow and a quiver of 20 arrows or a shortsword. A burglar's pack, a dungeoneer's pack, or an explorer's pack. Leather armour, two daggers, and thieves' tools.
Funds: 4d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Sorcerer


Sorcerers are spellcasters who draw on inherent magic from a gift or bloodline.
Hit dice: 1d6 per sorcerer level.
Hit points at first level: 6+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d6(or 4)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows.
Saving throws: Constitution and charisma.
Skills: Choose two from arcana, deception, insight, intimidation, persuasion, and religion.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Cantrips and spells: At 1st level you know four cantrips, two spells and have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: A light crossbow and 20 bolts or any simple weapon. A component pouch or an arcane focus. A dungeoneer's pack or an explorer's pack. Two daggers.
Funds: 3d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Warlock


Warlocks are wielders of magic that is derived from a bargain with an extraplanar entity.
Hit dice: 1d8 per warlock level.
Hit points at first level: 8+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8(or5)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: Light armour, simple weapons.
Saving throws: Wisdom and charisma
Skills: Choose two from arcana, deception, history, intimidation, investigation, nature and religion.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your charisma modifier.
Cantrips and spells: At 1st level you know two cantrips, 2 spells and have one 1st-level spell slot.
Equipment: A light crossbow and 20 bolts or any simple weapon. A component pouch or an arcane focus. A scholar's pack or a dungeoneer's pack. Leather armour, any simple weapon and two daggers.
Funds: 4d4 x 10 = amount of gold pieces.

Wizard


Wizards are scholarly magic-users capable of manipulating the structures of reality.
Hit dice: 1d6 per wizard level.
Hit points at first level: 6+ your constitution modifier.
Hit points at higher levels: 1d6(or 4)+ your constitution modifier.
Proficiencies: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows.
Saving throws: Intelligence and wisdom.
Skills: Choose two from arcana, history, insight, investigation, medicine, and religion.
Spell save DC: 8 + your proficiency bonus + your intelligence modifier.
Spell attack modifier: your proficiency bonus + your intelligence modifier.
Cantrips and spells: At 1st level you know three cantrips and have two 1st-level spell slots.
Equipment: A quarterstaff or a dagger. A component pouch or an arcane focus. A scholar's pack or an explorer's pack. A spellbook.
Funds: 4d4 x 10 =amount of gold pieces.
